La Cava Del Tequila
MCO
Disney World Epcot
Mexican Bars
Yelp
Google
Posted March 2, 2021

Summary

This is located in the Mexican pavilion at Epcot. When you enter the pyramid, there’s a little store to the right next to the fountain. In this COVID environment, there are social distancing lines as you enter here. Unfortunately you can’t sit inside the bar, so you have to buy the drink and sit outside. I do not know if (or how much of) the drinks are pre-mixed, but it was served super fast.

This is the black magic which was created by Neil Patrick Harris! It has Montelobos mezcal, blackberry purée, LeJay black currant liqueur, lime, and mint. Believe it or not, this was actually really delicious. Not sure if it was $21 delicious, but I would love to come back and try the other margaritas. It reminded me a little bit of Jamba Juice but with alcohol. The tequila was super smooth with all the different flavors.

Final Verdict

The drink we got was very good, and we would like to come back to try others.

Yelp Jabs

Prices are insane (even for Disney) for what you get. The bar itself was dirty, sticky, and messy. We won’t be back.

The prices are definitely not cheap, but I wonder about the dirty, stick, and messy part of it. I would imagine most bars would be like this.
