Posted March 2, 2021

Summary

This shop is located in the British area of Epcot. After you enter the park, steer left, and it’s your very first shop on the right. What’s nice about this area is there are some tables and seats that overlook the center fountain of Epcot. On a nice cool day, it’s quite pleasant to snack on this and enjoy the scenery. Keep in mind, we went when the park had 35% attendance, so it was way more spacious and enjoyable. Your miles may vary.

This was the fish and chips. The batter was surprisingly very crispy with a nice thickness to it. The fish itself was flaky, fresh, and delicious. We loaded lots of malt vinegar and tartar sauce, and it was good. The fries didn’t have too much specialness to it, but it was crispy on the outside and very fluffy on the inside. In the Yelp pictures, people seemed to have received 2 pieces, but we received 1 gigantic piece.

Final Verdict

The fish and chips is legit and pretty delicious as a shareable snack.

Yelp Jabs

Unfortunately, sometimes they come out so over-cooked that they are on the verge of being inedible. My experience has been that the later has a higher probability if you get the fish very late in the day. Maybe it has to do with the age of the frying oil.

We were some of the first guests to visit this booth, and it turned out well for us. But I can totally see them sacrificing quality when there’s a huge crowd. We were very lucky that we didn’t have to deal with that.
