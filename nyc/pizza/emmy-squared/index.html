Summary

I’ve been here numerous times to both the East Village and Manhattan locations, and they have consistently delivered pretty well within expectations.

The fried chicken sandwich is delectable. It was really good and pretty spicy with the right kick.

The Le Big Matt is like pretty big too. The cheese is super melty which makes it a little messy but pretty delicious.

The Roni Supreme is by far my favorite. It’s exactly what the description says. Be warned, it’s a little on the salty side.

The Emmy was surprisingly really good even though there isn’t meat on there.

The Margarita was pretty normal, so I would definitely add meat or something to it.

I’m generally not a fan of white pies, so I haven’t really gotten any here yet.

Final Verdict

This is a consistent Detroit hipster style pizza joint. The pepperoni pizza here is one of my favorites in the city. Occasionally service gets slammed, but it might be in people’s best interest to not be part of the zerg in going at core hours (and this also applies for food places in general).
