This little spot closed to the edge of Flatiron is a decent place if you're into Shanghainese food and are not close to Chinatown. The passion fruit lemonade was good. This apparently is one of Jean Georges Vongerichten's upscale farm-fresh eatery, and it has a Latin American fusion influence. We came here without reservations on a weekday night and were seated at the communal table. The spring pea guacamole with warm crunchy tortillas was actually pretty good. The chips themselves were nicely salted, and the big tortilla that came with the guacamole was more crunchy than the latter.
