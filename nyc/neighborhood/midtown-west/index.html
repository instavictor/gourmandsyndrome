This is probably one of my favorite Brazilian spots. The food all in all was pretty good, as well as some of the drinks. Typically, we order caipirinhas. But they had something called batidas. Batidas basically substitute the white sugar for condensed milk. It was interesting and good to me. Every dish comes with a side of rice, beans, salsa, farofa, yucca fried chips, and hot sauce.
