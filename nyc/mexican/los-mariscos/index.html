Summary

This is located in the backside of Chelsea Market along 14th Street. Basically walk towards Tippler, and it’d be on your right side. Can’t really miss it. It seems to be a semi-throwback to Baja style fish tacos.

I got shrimp ceviche, 2 fish tacos, and 1 spicy shrimp taco.

I wanted the fish ceviche, but they were out for whatever reason. The shrimp ceviche was pretty good, although I’m not too much of a fan of it being put on a big tostada shell. I thought it’d be more peruvian style but who would have thought.

The fish tacos were pretty good. I liked the sauce, and the taste of everything. My only complaint here was that if you don’t literally eat it instantly, the tortilla will break. I wonder if it should be double wrapped or not.

The spicy shrimp taco was also pretty good. I think I’d go for the fish taco next time though.

The prices here are all pretty expensive compared to what you can get in California. Taste wise, it’s all pretty good, but I don’t think I’ve ever been overly impressed. The seating is a bit of a mess as it’s a blend of tourists and locals. The tourists of course have all their bags and take their time, whereas the locals kind of just eat and go. In the seats, you will find bags of tostadas that lay around, so that random people can snack on. I thought about it for a second, but I didn’t want to think about what types of people were grabbing these chips.

Overall Impression

Just like Los Tacos, this place is probably one of the better fish taco places in New York City.

Yelp Jabs

I’ve finally got to cross this place of my list! I’ve visited twice and the food just gets better and better. The place is a true hidden gem.
