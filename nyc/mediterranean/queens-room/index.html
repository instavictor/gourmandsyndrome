Summary

This is a place along Ditmars Blvd in Astoria, relatively close to Taverna Kyclades. There is a lot of interesting outdoor setup on this street. The menu itself appears fairly simple, but this place had been highly recommended to us for the easy going vibe. For this COVID environment, the tables are organized north to south along the side street instead of the main street of Ditmars. It definitely felt a little safer.

Here are 2 good sized glasses of drinks: hazelnut chocolate milk and iced latte. Nothing particularly special about lunch, except they weren’t stingy with portion size.

The tuna salad sandwich came with red onions, celery, parsley, mayo, dijon, lemon, multigrain bread, and salad. The tuna was very compact, so it might be dry for some. However, my partner really enjoyed it for the fresh vegetables. The side salad was doused with a sharp vinaigrette.

The “Beyond” burger came with vegan garlic aioli, tomato, greens, red onion on a brioche bun and hand cut fries. I didn’t have any of this, but it was mentioned that Beyond burger meat is typically more dry than Impossible meat. Nonetheless, it’s not something I would go, but alas there are vegetarian options!

The chubby chicken came with breaded chicken breast, cheddar cheese, bacon, pickled red onion, buttermilk cole slaw on a brioche bun and hand cut fries. I had this one, and it’s no spicy chicken sandwich. However, it’s quite tasty for this place. I was debating between this burger and the QR burger, but I am glad I got this one. The chicken breast was juicy and the bread had a nice crunch to it. One ask that I have would be to make a spicy version of this.

The vibe definitely is pretty easy going on a holiday. Price wise, it’s not expensive at all, and the tables are spaced out very well.

Final Verdict

This is a very decent lunch place to get the basics on in Astoria. Can’t really go wrong especially if you’re on a budget.
