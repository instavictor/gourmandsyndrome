Posted October 19, 2019

Summary

We were just around the block looking to meet a friend for a drink and some bar food at a nearby restaurant. Turns out that restaurant was booked due to a football game, so we had to hunt for another one. This restaurant for whatever reason was dead quiet but opened at the time.

The cured Long Island duck breast came with pomegranate molasses, collard greens, and kabocha squash purée. The duck breast was super tender and crispy. My only gripe was that it was very salty.

The french dip came with gruyère, caramelized onion, horseradish aioli, and miso au jus. The sandwich itself was fairly mediocre at best. I didn’t taste much of the horseradish aioli. The miso au jus had a slight offputting flavor to it and was overly salty.

Final Verdict

The food is pretty mediocre at best and is also very salty. There are probably better restaurants around the area for similar New American bar food. Looking at the Yelp reviews, it seems most of their positive clientele are out of town folks that are just looking for that upscale bar atmosphere.

Yelp Jabs

As a vegetarian, my options were quite limited!

It sounds no different than other restaurants with limited vegetarian options.
