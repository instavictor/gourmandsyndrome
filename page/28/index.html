I heard good raves about Dino BBQ from other people. The pickling of the salad is pretty good in general. In the trio of meat, you will find pork belly, chicken, and brisket. The brisket to me was just ok. I think Hometown was much better. The pork belly was also ok. Surprisingly, I liked the chicken the best. It was tender and full of flavor and not overcooked. The collard greens were not memorable and neither was the cornbread.

We’re boarding in Girdwood, and at night we’re looking for a good restaurant to try out. Surprisingy, Girdwood has some pretty decent finds here. The fried sweet potatoes was nice. In Anchorage, there is this spot that for some reason got a lot of good Yelp reviews. Hirohisa apparently has been around for a while, but they just received a Michelin star again this year. With that in mind, I felt the price point $160 pp for the 9-course tasting + $70 sake pairing was a decent price for splurging for a special someone’s birthday dinner.
