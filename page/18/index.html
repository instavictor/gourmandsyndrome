May 5, 2019
The ambiance and decor of this place is pretty interesting. On Sundays, they have live cuban music playing which is pretty nice. The virgin passion fruit drink was pretty good. It had no alcohol of course, but it came with a sugarcane in it. The juice was not too sweet, and tasted decently fresh. The ceviche de pargo was surprisingly pretty good. I’m normally not a fan of ceviche barring a few places that do it extremely well.
