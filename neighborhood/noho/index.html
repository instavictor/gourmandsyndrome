I’ve walked by here many times, but I’ve never actually ate here until now. One of our guests loves this place because of the burger. The prime beef tartare was pretty good. It definitely was tender and tasted like good quality beef. The rotisserie chicken was really tender. If I had to dock it points, it’d be for how salty the sauce and everything was.
