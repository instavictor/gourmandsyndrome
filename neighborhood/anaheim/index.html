Updated April 14, 2019
In our trip to Disneyland, we made a reservation here a month in advance on a Monday night thinking there will be a lot of people. Surprisingly (or unsurprisingly), there was practically no one in here. The dining room was pretty sparse. The bread selection was great. I loved the sourdough a lot, and how it all came out pretty hot. The grilled diver scallop was good too.
