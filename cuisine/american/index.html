We’re boarding in Girdwood, and at night we’re looking for a good restaurant to try out. Surprisingy, Girdwood has some pretty decent finds here. The fried sweet potatoes was nice. I prefer this over thin cut sweet potato fries because you can get more fullness out of this. It was served with some jalapeno-ish creamy sauce that wasn’t spicy. I would’ve preferred it having more of a kick.
